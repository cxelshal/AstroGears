Iterative Design Process

Our team followed an iterative design process to create the optimal gauntlet prototype for rescue missions. We aimed to enhance the gauntlet's ergonomics, durability, and overall aesthetics throughout the development stages.

Key Steps in the Design Process:

  1. Conceptualization: Brainstorming ideas and defining design goals.
  2. Initial CAD Design: Creating 3D models of the gauntlet.
  3. Prototype Testing: Building and testing initial prototypes.
  4. Evaluation and Feedback: Gathering feedback from team members and potential users.
  5. Iterative Refinement: Making design improvements based on feedback and real-world scenarios.
  6. Final CAD Design: Developing the optimized 3D model for manufacturing.

Throughout the design process, we collaborated closely with the hardware and software team members to ensure seamless integration of sensors and components.
